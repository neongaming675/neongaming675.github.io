GTA SAN ANDREAS

About Game

Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is an iconic generation-defining masterpiece that revolutionized sandbox gameplay. Following the tragic murder of his mother, protagonist Carl "CJ" Johnson returns to a 1990s Los Santos torn apart by gang violence and corrupt police. Unlike previous titles, it delivers an expansive, state-wide journey across three massive, distinct cities, blending action, racing, and unprecedented life simulation mechanics. Players are immersed in a living, breathing digital playground where they can customize almost every aspect of CJ’s life, from building muscle at the gym and eating at fast-food joints to learning martial arts and engaging in turf wars.
